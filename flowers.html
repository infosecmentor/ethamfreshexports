PRODUCTS& SERVICES

Service Description:

Our Flower Export Service is a comprehensive solution designed to meet the global demand for a wide variety of exquisite flowers. We specialize in exporting flowers, as well as an extensive range of other flowers. Whether you're a retailer, event planner, or floral enthusiast, our service caters to your diverse flower export needs

CUT FLOWERS-We cultivate and export a wide variety of cut flowers, including roses, lilies, tulips, orchids, carnations, and more. Our flowers are meticulously grown under controlled conditions, ensuring exceptional quality, vibrant colours, and long-lasting freshness.

FLOWER BOUQUETS AND ARRANGEMENTS-We specialize in creating stunning bouquets and floral arrangements that showcase the natural elegance of our flowers. Our expert floral designers craft bespoke designs for weddings, events, celebrations, and corporate functions.

Etham Fresh Exports

Key Features:

Extensive Floral Variety: In addition to our stunning range of roses, our service offers an extensive selection of other flowers. This includes but is not limited to lilies, tulips, orchids, daisies, sunflowers, and more. Each type of flower is carefully cultivated for its unique characteristics, colors, and symbolism

Stringent Quality Control: Our commitment to quality extends to all flower varieties. Each bloom undergoes meticulous quality control checks throughout its journey, ensuring that only the freshest and most visually appealing flowers are included in our export shipments.

Customized Packaging: Presentation matters, and we understand that different occasions and markets may require tailored packaging. Our export service includes customizable packaging options, allowing you to align the presentation with your specific needs and branding.

Sustainability Focus: Ethical and environmentally responsible flower farming is at the core of our service. We prioritize eco-friendly cultivation methods and use sustainable packaging materials whenever possible to minimize our ecological impact.

Global Reach: Our Flower Export Service is not limited by borders. With experience in exporting flowers to various countries, we are well-versed in international phytosanitary and customs regulations to ensure seamless cross-border deliveries.

How It Works:

Order Placement: Customers can place orders for a wide range of flowers through our user-friendly online platform or by contacting our specialized export team. We provide prompt responses to inquiries and expert guidance in selecting the perfect blooms for specific markets, events, or occasions.

Quality Assurance: Upon receiving an order, our dedicated quality control experts meticulously select the finest flowers from our farms. These flowers are rigorously inspected for freshness, size, color, and overall health

Custom Packaging: Recognizing the importance of presentation, we collaborate with customers to determine their packaging preferences. Whether it's customized labels, quantities, or packaging designs, we accommodate individual branding and market specifications.

Export and Logistics: Our proficient logistics team oversees the entire export process, including compliance with export regulations and the efficient coordination of transportation. We handle all essential documentation to guarantee a smooth and trouble-free delivery.


Delivery: Our customers can expect reliable and punctual delivery of their selected flowers to their designated destinations. We take utmost care to preserve the freshness and quality of the flowers during transit.
