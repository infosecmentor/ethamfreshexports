Etham Flowers

PRODUCTS& SERVICES

Service Description:

Our produce is carefully selected and grown under controlled conditions to ensure exceptional taste, freshness, and nutritional value. We offer a wide range of fruits and vegetables, including avocados, mangoes, passion fruit, tomatoes, peppers, and more.

Exporting fruits and vegetables is a significant component of international trade in the agricultural sector. It involves the sourcing, packaging, and shipment of fresh fruits and vegetables from one country to another

Farm Selection: Exporters partner with local farmers or agricultural producers who grow specific fruits and vegetables suitable for export. These farms are often chosen based on their ability to meet quality and safety standards

Etham Fresh Exports

Key Features:

Crop Planning: Collaboration between exporters and growers includes planning crop cycles that align with seasonal demand in target markets. This ensures a consistent supply of fresh produce.

Quality Assurance: To meet export standards, fruits and vegetables are cultivated using good agricultural practices (GAP), which focus on soil health, reduced pesticide use, and high-quality produce

Timely Harvesting: Fruits and vegetables are harvested at their peak ripeness to ensure optimal flavor, texture, and appearance. Proper timing is essential to maintain the quality of the produce during transportation.


Packaging: Fruits and vegetables are packed in specialized packaging that protects them during transit. Packaging varies depending on the type of produce and specific market requirements.

How It Works:

Pre-Cooling: To maintain freshness, produce is pre-cooled to the appropriate temperature immediately after harvesting. This process slows down the ripening process. Cold Storage: Fruits and vegetables are stored in temperature-controlled facilities to extend their shelf life and preserve their quality until they are ready for shipment.

Phytosanitary Certification: Exported fruits and vegetables must adhere to the phytosanitary regulations of the importing country. Certificates are issued to confirm compliance with pest and disease control measures

Transportation Mode: Produce is transported by air, sea, or land, depending on the destination and urgency. Temperature-controlled containers are used to maintain optimal conditions during transit. Documentation: Exporters manage all necessary documentation, including customs forms, certificates of origin, and shipping manifests

Export and Logistics: Our proficient logistics team oversees the entire export process, including compliance with export regulations and the efficient coordination of transportation. We handle all essential documentation to guarantee a smooth and trouble-free delivery.


Delivery: Our customers can expect reliable and punctual delivery of their selected flowers to their designated destinations. We take utmost care to preserve the freshness and quality of the flowers during transit.
